Planet Facts & Characteristics

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun, known for its distinctive red color caused by iron oxide on its surface.

  • Distance from Earth: 54.6 to 401 million km
  • Surface Temperature: Average -65°C
  • Atmosphere: 95% carbon dioxide
  • Moons: Phobos and Deimos
