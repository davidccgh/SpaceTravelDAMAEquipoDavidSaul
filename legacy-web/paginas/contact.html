Space Hawkings is an educational platform dedicated to exploring the journey from Earth's exosphere to the lunar surface. We provide interactive content about space travel, orbital mechanics, and the science behind human spaceflight missions.

A typical journey from Earth to the Moon takes approximately 3 days. The Apollo missions took around 76 hours to reach lunar orbit, covering a distance of about 384,400 kilometers (238,855 miles).

A lunar mission consists of five main stages: Launch from Earth's surface, passing through the Exosphere (10,000 km altitude), Orbital Cruise in low Earth orbit, Trans-lunar injection and travel, and finally Lunar Arrival and landing.
